TJ or Tidjani is an actor and a film, theatre producer. He was the president for Culture Sports Association in Stockholm during 1986 - 1992. He was starred in the 1979 version of Nosferatu the Vampyre. In 1994, he was a dance choreographer for Swedish Film Institute in Stockholm. Tj is involved in the Human Rights and can speak chinese, french, english, arabic, swedish, norwegian, spanish and italian.
